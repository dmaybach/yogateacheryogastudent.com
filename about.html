In one sentence, what is YTYS?

A channel for yoga teachers and yoga students to connect and discover each other's practice, yoga friends, studios and progress.

What problem is YTYS solving?

Yoga Teachers:

  • 1 - YTs don't know who their true followers are.
  • 2 - YTs don't have a way to keep their followers up to day with their class schedule. And lastly,
  • 3 - YTs schedule is all over the place since they teach at several studios.

Yoga Students, on the other end:

  • 1- Don't have a complete "studio to studio" "24/7" schedule of their favorite teachers and
  • 2- 90% of them won't attend a random Yoga class with a sub.
  • 3- 100% started practicing yoga because they found a teacher they liked and followed.

What is YTYS solution?

Our mobile app where YTs can log in their daily schedule whether they teach in studio "A" or "B" therefore their students can know where they are teaching and when
