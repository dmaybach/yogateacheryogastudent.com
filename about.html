Welcome to YTYS - Yoga Teacher Yoga Student

Yoga doesn't suck, what makes Yoga amazing is the connection you have with your teacher. Whether it's the flow, music, voice, calmness, level of strength, yoga type, you name it. So what do you do with this amazing Yoga practice you have discovered thru this new Yoga Teacher?
you follow your teacher! you start looking at your Yoga studio schedule and go to your favorite teacher's classes. Along the way, you make new friends and try other teachers and you might start following them too. So that's the secret of Yoga and if you don't believe me, I dare you to ask any Yoga student out there if he or she will choose to spend a free hour of Yoga with a random Teacher if he/she could choose his/her favorite Teacher instead.
